# Papyrus Favicon Design

## Design Concept
- Ancient Egyptian scroll/papyrus motif with modern minimalism
- Clean lines suggesting document scrolls
- Gold/earth tones with white background
- Scalable from 16x16 to 512x512

## Color Palette
- Primary: #D4A574 (Muted gold - ancient papyrus)
- Secondary: #2C3E50 (Dark blue - trust/tech)
- Accent: #FFFFFF (White - clarity)
- Shadow: #1A1F2E (Dark blue - depth)

## Design Elements
- Central scroll shape
- Subtle hieroglyphic markings
- Clean geometric borders
- Professional rounded corners

## Technical Requirements
- Transparent background
- High contrast for visibility
- Works at all sizes
- Modern yet timeless aesthetic
