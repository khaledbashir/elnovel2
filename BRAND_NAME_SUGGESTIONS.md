## Selection Criteria
- **Memorable**: Easy to recall and spell
- **Professional**: Suitable for business context
- **Scalable**: Works from startup to enterprise
- **Tech-Forward**: Reflects AI capabilities
- **Domain Available**: Check .com/.io/.app availability
- **Brand Story**: Has meaning behind the name
