# Papyrus - AI-Powered Document Management Platform

## Project Overview
Papyrus is a versatile, AI-powered document management platform designed to transform how businesses create, collaborate on, and manage various types of documents. Moving beyond the traditional Statement of Work focus, this platform serves as a comprehensive solution for modern document workflows.

## Core Concept
ElNovel2 leverages advanced AI technology (Tambo AI integration) to provide intelligent document assistance, automated content generation, and smart collaboration features. The platform supports multiple document types including contracts, proposals, reports, invoices, meeting notes, and custom document formats.

## Logo Design Direction
The logo should reflect:
- **Ancient Heritage + Modern Tech**: Blend of historical documentation with cutting-edge AI
- **Intelligence & AI**: Modern, tech-forward appearance
- **Collaboration**: Elements suggesting teamwork and connectivity
- **Versatility**: Design that works across professional contexts
- **Professionalism**: Clean, trustworthy aesthetic suitable for business use
- **Timelessness**: Design that suggests durability and longevity

Consider color schemes that convey trust (blues), intelligence (purples/teals), innovation (gradient effects), and heritage (gold/earth tones). The design should incorporate subtle papyrus or scroll elements while maintaining scalability from app icons to full branding. The logo should balance ancient Egyptian minimalism with modern clean aesthetics.
